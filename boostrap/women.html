Women's collection

Salwar Kameez

For a casual look, opt for a simple and relaxed kameez design. This could include straight-cut kameez, A-line kameez, or even short kurtas, depending on personal preferences and the latest fashion trends.

Anarkali

While traditional Anarkali dresses adhere to classic designs, modern variations may incorporate contemporary elements, such as unconventional sleeve styles, or fusion with Western fashion trends.

Patiala Pants

The hallmark of Patiala pants is the pleats and gathers around the waist, which create volume and give the pants their distinctive drape. The pleats are often stitched down to maintain the structured appearance.

Wedding Blouse

Wedding blouses feature intricate embroidery and embellishments to enhance their beauty.Common embellishments include stones,and thread work. The type of embellishments chosen can significantly impact the overall look of the blouse.

Kids Tradional

kids School Uniform

Children's uniforms should be designed for ease of wear. This includes features such as elastic waistbands, adjustable straps, and easy-to-fasten buttons or zippers. Such elements make it convenient for children to dress themselves .
